Help Documentation

Introduction

Welcome to the Protein Tracking App! This app helps you track your protein and calorie intake, set daily goals, and monitor your progress.

Main Features:

  • Meal Logging: Easily log your meals with details on protein content and calories.
  • Goal Setting: Set and update your daily protein and calorie goals.
  • Progress Tracking: View your progress with detailed charts and graphs.

Getting Started

Creating an Account

To create an account, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the Sign-Up page from the navigation bar.
  2. Fill out the form with your email, first name, and password.
  3. Click the "Sign Up" button to create your account.

Logging In

To log in, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Login page from the navigation bar.
  2. Enter your email and password.
  3. Click the "Login" button to access your account.

Navigating the App

Use the navigation bar to access the main sections of the app:

  • Home: Log meals, view daily intake, and set goals.
  • Dashboard: View detailed visualizations of your progress.
  • Data: Manage user profile information and view overall progress.
